# Relationship Rules

All relationship outputs preserve the order in which people were added to their family branch.

## Paternal-Uncle

Father's brothers.

Example: Remus's father is Ted. Ted has no known brothers, so Remus has no paternal uncles.

## Maternal-Uncle

Mother's brothers.

Example: Remus's mother is Victoire. Victoire's brother is Louis, so Remus's maternal uncle is Louis.

## Paternal-Aunt

Father's sisters.

Example: William's father is James. James's sister is Lily, so William's paternal aunt is Lily.

## Maternal-Aunt

Mother's sisters.

Example: Remus's mother is Victoire. Victoire's sisters are Dominique and any later daughters added through Flora.

## Sister-In-Law

Includes spouse's sisters and wives of siblings.

Example: Lily's siblings are James and Albus. Their wives are Darcy and Alice, so Lily's sisters-in-law are Darcy and Alice.

## Brother-In-Law

Includes spouse's brothers and husbands of siblings.

Example: Victoire's siblings include Dominique and Louis. If Dominique had a husband, he would be Victoire's brother-in-law.

## Son

Male children of the requested person.

Example: Ginerva's sons are James and Albus.

## Daughter

Female children of the requested person.

Example: Ginerva's daughter is Lily.

## Siblings

All children of the same known parent except the requested person.

Example: Minerva is added as Flora's child after Victoire, Dominique, and Louis. Minerva's siblings are Victoire, Dominique, and Louis.
